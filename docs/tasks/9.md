# Searches are to samey
Looking at the reports generated after each run, the searches all seem to be very similar from run to run. They all seem to just be variations on a very few themes:

```
HOTA Gold Coast what's on June 2026 music
Miami Marketta Gold Coast upcoming gigs May June 2026
Burleigh Bazaar live music events June 2026
Coolangatta Hotel gig guide May June 2026
Star Gold Coast venue events tickets June 2026
Fortitude Music Hall Brisbane upcoming shows June 2026
The Triffid Brisbane event calendar May June 2026
Brisbane open mic nights and jam sessions June 2026
```

The prompt to the LLM should be generating a lot more variability, and the format of the searches should vary a lot more than this.

## Investigate
How are the search terms being generated by the prompts? Why are they so similar from run to run? The idea of the eintire app is to generate a lot of variation, thereby reaching into the corners of the web to eventually find events that would otherwise be hard to find.
